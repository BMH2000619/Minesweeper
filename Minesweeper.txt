Idea: Minesweeper

Pseudocode:
- Create a board that has a certain number of squared cells.
- Fill the cells with mines and safe and numbered cells.
- When a cell has a mine, adjust the count of the adjacent cells.
- Left click to place a flag on a cell.
- Right click to reveal what is hidden behind the chosen cell.
- Check for a win or lose conditions.
- If win, reveal all mines and show win, else reveal all mines and show lose.
